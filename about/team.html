Joining the MyBB Team

Interested in joining the MyBB team? It all starts by getting involved with the community such as through development or support.

Get Involved →

The MyBB team is composed of volunteers committed to developing, supporting, and managing the MyBB forum software project. They have a single mission: to make the best forum software possible.

All members have a Team group assigned, which indicates their main focus within the Project.

All Team changes follow the Personnel management guide.
